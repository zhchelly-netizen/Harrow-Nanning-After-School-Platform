studentswhodemonstrateoutstandingleadershipabilities,serviceawareness,andsocialresponsibility
in school or community projects. This scoring model adopts a multi-dimensional evaluation system,
comprehensively assessing students' performance in leadership positions, project commitment, social
impact,andpresentation.
